Our Story

The Secret to Feeling Beautiful

At Ivy's Beauty Secret, beauty is more than a service — it's a feeling of confidence, comfort and indulgence we create for every woman who walks through our doors.

Born from a Love of Beauty & Care

Ivy's Beauty Secret was founded with one vision — to give the women of Abu Dhabi a salon and spa that feels as premium as the world's finest beauty destinations, right here in Musaffah.

We believe every woman deserves a space to slow down, be pampered and rediscover her confidence. That's why we combine expert craftsmanship with warm, personal hospitality and an environment designed for pure relaxation.

From your first consultation to the final reveal in the mirror, our promise is the same: Be a Pampered Princess.
